A video player built on a native `<video>` element: play/pause, an interactive
seekbar, a custom volume slider, a playback-speed menu, fullscreen, keyboard
shortcuts and full a11y. Analytics, watch-% milestones and forward-seek
restriction are built in — enabled with plain props.

## Keyboard shortcuts

While the player (or a non-input descendant) holds focus:

- `Space` — play / pause
- `←` / `→` — seek ∓5s (forward seeks respect `restrictForwardSeek`)
- `↑` / `↓` — volume ∓10%
- `M` — mute / unmute
- `F` — toggle fullscreen

The seekbar and volume slider own `Arrow` / `Home` / `End` while focused.

## Built-in behaviours

These props turn on behaviour previously implemented by hand in the trainings
player. The parent receives playback information through the callbacks:

- **`onTrackAction`** — called on play, on pause, and every 5 min during
  continuous playback. Use it to drive analytics / session keep-alive.
- **`onMilestone(milestone, video)`** — called once when watched-% first crosses
  `25`, `50` and `75`. For progress-funnel analytics.
- **`onComplete(video)`** — called once when the video is "watched enough": the
  remaining time drops to `min(10s, 3% of duration)` — i.e. the later of "last
  10s" and "97%". Short videos complete at 97%; long videos in their final 10s.
- **`restrictForwardSeek`** — prevents seeking past the furthest-watched point and
  renders a marker at that position.

## Notes

- Advanced browser behaviours are always disabled: the native context menu (and
  its "Save video as…" download), Picture-in-Picture, remote playback and drag.
  This is intentional for embedded product video; there is no opt-in for v1.
- The playback-speed menu portals into the player wrapper (not `document.body`)
  so it stays visible and interactive in fullscreen.
